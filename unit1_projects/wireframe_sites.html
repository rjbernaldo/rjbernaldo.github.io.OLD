Wireframing

Google.com

This webpage is trying to present information based on the user's query. Not much visual challenges as users are greeted with a very simple & elegant search box at first glance. Further options are found on the upper-right part of the page.

Soundcloud.com

This website is trying to bridge the gap between music artists & listeners. I love this website! It is simple enough to understand on the first usage, and complex enough for advanced users looking for more features or information.

Youtube.com

This website is severely changing the game in terms of media consumption. It presents the data (or video) to users in a simple manner, and at the same time, inform the user of related data using the sidebar.
