Dealing with Conflict

  • Discuss a time you had an emotional conflict with someone. What was it about? What happened?

    One particular event that comes to mind is when my direct manager in my previous work was restraining me from doing more interesting work.

  • What was the source of the conflict?

    Source of the conflict was miscommunication

  • How did you handle that conflict?

    I wanted to know why he wasn't allowing me to tackle more interesting projects. I later realized that there are just some stuff that needs to be finished in my department before I could try out new stuff.

  • Did your actions make the conflict better or worse?

    I believe my actions made the resolved the conflict, for both parties.

  • If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?

    I wouldn't have waited a long time to talk to him. I should have just confronted him the moment he said no. I feel that I wasted time overthinking the situation.

  • What did you learn from this experience?

    As with most conflict with other people, all you need to do is communicate.
