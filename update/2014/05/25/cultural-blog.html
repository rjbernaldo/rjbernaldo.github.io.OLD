Fireside Chat

Based on the video, what's your take on the DBC experience?

Wow, the video was so beautiful. I've always dreamt about being a software engineer so I can finally become part of the whole tech industry. I've watched movies where they form a company from their basement and change the whole world with what they create. I've always envisioned myself making a product that will be so awesome, people will be so happy to use it. But when Shereef mentioned that DBC is actually an experiment on it's own, and we are it's guinea pigs, I felt a sense of satisfaction. I felt like, wow, I am finally contributing something to the tech industry by being part of this one big experiment where people learn Ruby on Rails.

What is your impression of DBC?

DBC, for me, is more than just a school now. It made a lot of sense when Shereef talked about the 2 mindsets when coming in: the consumer mindset, and the creator mindset. For me, DBC is an experiment where people want to make themselves better. It is an experiment that aims to change the tech industry by giving birth to software engineers who are capable to perform, and are hungry to learn even more.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I honestly am just so happy to be a part of this culture right now. I have always tackled tech alone, I know I love it, but sometimes, it gets lonely. I look forward to being with people that want to succeed.

Rj Bernaldo

Student at Dev Boot Camp
