Issues in Tech

  • Identify a problem facing the tech world

    One problem is the fact that even though web applications can be made to be used globally, there are still regional restrictions before it can become succesfull around the world.

  • Explain why it is a problem and some background

    Not all people in countries outside of the US are willing to spend money online. A lot of people from my country (Philippines) still prefer to buy from real shops and choose to get the free stuff (in terms of software) instead of spending online.

    This is due to the fact that people still do not trust the internet. I just feel that if the attitude of everyone in the world changes, technology can advance so much faster.

  • Hypothesize about potential solutions

    Best way around this is to teach them. Give them a reliable service they can't ignore.
